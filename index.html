DAY 4

DAY 1

Subic, Olongapo

Pagasa Weather Station

Weather?

At Pagasa Weather Station, we have gain knowledge on how instruments help monitor the weather, gather data, and analyze weather patterns.

First Aviation Academy

EROPLANO? SAAN?

A revolution in resolution. At First Aviation Academy, we observed a student flying an aircraft using the REDBIRD MCX simulator.

Vessel Traffic Management System

VESSEL MONITORING?

The last stop of our DAY-1 Tour was at Vessel Traffic Management, where we obtained knowledge on how to optimize traffic flow, protect marine environments, and support efficient port operations through advanced monitoring, coordination, and regulatory measures

DAY 2

Makati, Pasig

QBO INNOVATION HUB ANY IDEA?

At QBO Innovation Hub, we gain knowledge about how the platform serves as support for entrepreneurs with resources, mentorship, and networking opportunities. However, QBO doesn't stop there; it also fosters collaborations among startups, helping them scale and compete internationally.

PACKETWORX IOT Devices?

Our visit to Packetworx was an eye-opening experience as we witnessed how IoT devices can revolutionize industries, helping businesses make smarter decisions using data and improving their operational efficiency..

DAY 3

Makati, Manila

LIGHT TRAIL TRANSIT AUTHORITY LINE-2

UY TRAIN?

Our visit to the Light Rail Transit Authority was enlightening. We experienced the modernity and sophistication of LRTA's infrastructure and even stepped onto the platform for free.

National Museum of the Philippines

MUSEUM YARN?

Walking through the National Museum was a fascinating experience. It felt like stepping back in time, surrounded by art, artifacts, and stories that captured the essence of our identity. The museum was filled with a treasure trove of artifacts and exhibits that offered insights into our rich cultural heritage.

DAY 4

Makati, Quezon City

98 LABS, INC 15+ YEARS DEVELOPMENT

As the President/CEO discussed with us, 98labs is a prominent software development company committed to fostering a culture of innovation, collaboration, and client-centricity. This dedication makes them a trusted partner in driving digital transformation across various industries.

HYTEC POWER INC IOT Devices?

Our last visit was at Hytec Power Inc., where we encountered numerous technologies and equipment used across various industries.
